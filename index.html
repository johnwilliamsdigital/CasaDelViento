The wind makes the wine at Casa del Viento.

When the summer sun sets & the temperature cools, the breeze atop the northern Sierra foothills avails our Syrah vinyard. This signature process precludes premature heat, & induced sugars. This uinque process endows Casa del Viento vino, with an unmatched depth of character and complexity.

Don't Take Our Word For It...

Glen Dilley

The terroir (soils, sun, climate, wind) found at Casa Del Viento are not only perfect for the Syrah grape cultivated at Casa Del Viento. However, perched atop cliffs overlooking a vast canyon of the northern Sierra Nevada foothills, Casa Del Viento's true charm is what it does for the soul.

Jonathan & Yolanda

Casa del Viento sits beautifully atop Bidwell Upper Park, overlooking the canyon below. Its location is perfect for watching glorious sunsets. This rustic and intimate setting makes Casa del Viento a romantic choice for those looking to relax into a picturesque getaway.
